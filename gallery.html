Tulips in Minnesota Landscape Arboretum

Every year, a field of colorful tulips becomes the highlight of the season at the Minnesota Landscape Arboretum. Most tulips bloom at the Minnesota Landscape Arboretum in mid-May. The Arboretum has 45 plant collections and more than 5,000 plant species and varieties. The flowers can remain in bloom for two weeks or longer. I took these photos on Saturday May 8, 2021.
